Mitek

Mitek is a software tool used by truss designers to create structural roof support systems. They use it to export trusses, apply loads, optimize webs, splices, and check quantities. They also design roof systems, prepare engineering calculations, and lay out trusses using Mitek. Some truss designers are even certified in Mitek Canadian Engineering.

Here's how truss designers use mitek:

  • Designed the structural roof support systems for hundreds of homes, churches and businesses using MiTek CAD truss engineering software.
  • Export trusses into MiTek Engineering program applying loads, optimize webs lengths and splices, check quantities.

    • Design Software

    Design software helps create digital models of structures and systems. Truss designers use design software like Truswal Intellibuild, Simpson Build, and AutoCAD to create 3-D models of new construction projects. They also use design software to design roof and floor truss systems using blueprints and building plans.

    Here's how truss designers use design software:

  • Maintained certification on design software.
  • Designed roof and floor truss systems using blueprints and building plans with various 3-D design software programs.

    • Auto CAD

    Auto CAD is a computer-aided design software used for creating, modifying, analyzing, and optimizing digital models. Truss designers use Auto CAD to create detailed layouts for projects, design structural components and equipment foundations, and develop construction documents. They also utilize the software to ensure the accuracy and completeness of structural drawings.

    Here's how truss designers use auto cad:

  • Created detailed layouts for projects using AUTO CAD software Used company specific software to create reports for production and delivery
  • Designed Hydraulic Cylinders for Manufacturing Worked with Auto CAD in design application.

    • Layout Design

    Layout design is the process of creating a visual representation of a product, system, or facility. Truss designers use layout design to prepare systems according to customer drawings and to download online data to AutoCAD, providing notes and enhancements as required.

    Here's how truss designers use layout design:

  • Download the On-Line Data layout design to AutoCAD and provide notes and enhancements to the layout as required.
  • Prepare layout design systems according to customer drawings provided by the sales team.

    • Roof Trusses

    Roof trusses are structural components that support roofs and floors in buildings. Truss designers use roof trusses in their job by designing and engineering them for residential, commercial, and industrial projects, ensuring they meet state-specific snow load requirements. They also create layouts and estimates for roof trusses and manage the Truss Department. As one truss designer put it, "Designed and engineered roof trusses per design and specifications."

    Here's how truss designers use roof trusses:

  • Designed and Engineered Roof Trusses per Design and Specifications
  • Designed and estimated floor trusses, roof trusses, hangers, and beams for residential and commercial buildings.
